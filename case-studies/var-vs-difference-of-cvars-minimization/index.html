CASE STUDY SUMMARYn Problems 1 minimized VaR using standard PSG risk function VaR_Risk. Let us denote the confidence level of VaR by alpha in Problem 1. Problem 2 minimized the difference of two CVaR functions with confidence levels alpha_1 and alpha_2 satisfying inequalities alpha_1 < alpha < alpha_2. Confidence levels alpha_1 and alpha_2 are close to alpha.
